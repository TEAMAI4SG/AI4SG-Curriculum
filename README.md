## 🗂️ Curriculum Overview

The AI4SG curriculum engages undergraduate students from **all disciplines** to propose or develop AI-powered solutions addressing pressing community issues aligned with the UN SDGs. It has been adopted across **3 universities**, **4 disciplines**, and has engaged **2,500+ undergraduate students**.

The curriculum is structured around two intervention levels:

---

## Level 1 — Ideation

Students identify a real social problem aligned with a UN SDG, explore relevant AI technologies, and propose an AI-powered solution through research, design thinking, and team presentation. No coding required.

➡️ [AI4SG Mini Project (Ideation)](https://github.com/TEAMAI4SG/AI4SG_mini_project)
