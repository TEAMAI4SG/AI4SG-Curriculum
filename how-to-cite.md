# How to Cite the AI4SG Curriculum

If you use the AI4SG curriculum in your course, program, or research, 
please cite the publication most relevant to your use.

---

## APA Format

**Full pedagogy framework:**
Chen, Y., Albert, L. J., & Macias, H. (2023). Prototyping AI-powered 
social innovation in an undergraduate MIS course. *International 
Conference on Information Systems (ICIS) 2023.*

**AI ethics/debate module:**
Chen, Y., Jensen, S., Roldan, M., & Harper, S. (2023). Pro or con? 
Introducing AI ethics debates in an undergraduate MIS course. 
*The American Conference on Information Systems (AMCIS) 2023.*

**Introductory MIS module:**
Chen, Y., & Hill, T. (2023). AI for Social Good (AI4SG) education 
in an undergraduate introductory MIS course. *The American Conference 
on Information Systems (AMCIS) 2023.*

---

## BibTeX Format

**Full pedagogy framework:**
```bibtex
@inproceedings{chen2023prototyping,
  title={Prototyping AI-Powered Social Innovation in an Undergraduate MIS Course},
  author={Chen, Yu and Albert, Leslie J. and Macias, Heather},
  booktitle={International Conference on Information Systems (ICIS)},
  year={2023}
}
```

**AI ethics/debate module:**
```bibtex
@inproceedings{chen2023procon,
  title={Pro or Con? Introducing AI Ethics Debates in an Undergraduate MIS Course},
  author={Chen, Yu and Jensen, Scott and Roldan, Malu and Harper, Shannon},
  booktitle={The American Conference on Information Systems (AMCIS)},
  year={2023}
}
```

**Introductory MIS module:**
```bibtex
@inproceedings{chen2023ai4sg,
  title={AI for Social Good (AI4SG) Education in an Undergraduate Introductory MIS Course},
  author={Chen, Yu and Hill, Timothy},
  booktitle={The American Conference on Information Systems (AMCIS)},
  year={2023}
}
```
