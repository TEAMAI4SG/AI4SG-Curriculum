# How to Cite the AI4SG Curriculum

Please cite the publication(s) most relevant to your use of the curriculum.

---

## Level 1 — Ideation

### APA Format

Chen, Y., Granco, G., Hou, Y., Macias, H., & Gomez, F. A. (2024). 
AI for Social Good education at Hispanic Serving Institutions. 
*Proceedings of the AAAI Symposium Series, 3.*

Chen, Y., & Hill, T. (2023). AI for Social Good (AI4SG) education 
in an undergraduate introductory MIS course. *The American Conference 
on Information Systems (AMCIS) 2023.*

Chen, Y., Albert, L. J., & Jensen, S. (2021). Innovation Farm: 
Teaching Artificial Intelligence through gamified social 
entrepreneurship in an introductory MIS course. *Decision Sciences 
Journal of Innovative Education.*

### BibTeX Format

```bibtex
@inproceedings{chen2024ai4sg,
  title={AI for Social Good Education at Hispanic Serving Institutions},
  author={Chen, Yu and Granco, Gabriel and Hou, Yunfei and Macias, Heather and Gomez, Frank A.},
  booktitle={Proceedings of the AAAI Symposium Series},
  volume={3},
  year={2024}
}

@inproceedings{chen2023ai4sg,
  title={AI for Social Good (AI4SG) Education in an Undergraduate Introductory MIS Course},
  author={Chen, Yu and Hill, Timothy},
  booktitle={The American Conference on Information Systems (AMCIS)},
  year={2023}
}

@article{chen2021innovation,
  title={Innovation Farm: Teaching Artificial Intelligence through Gamified Social Entrepreneurship in an Introductory MIS Course},
  author={Chen, Yu and Albert, Leslie Jordan and Jensen, Scott},
  journal={Decision Sciences Journal of Innovative Education},
  year={2021}
}
```
